# Basic Electricity

This chapter is designed for secondary school students. It works with three very common circuit situations:

- resistors in series
- resistors in parallel
- mixed circuits

## Goals

By the end of this block, students should be able to:

1. identify how resistors are connected
2. calculate equivalent resistance in simple cases
3. apply Ohm's law to find current or voltage
4. explain the process clearly and in order

## Before you start

Quick reminder:

- Ohm's law: $V = I \cdot R$
- in series: resistances are added
- in parallel: reciprocals are added

```{admonition} Secondary-level tip
:class: tip

Before calculating, it helps to draw the circuit and write down the known values. That avoids many mistakes.
```
